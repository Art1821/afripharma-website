About AfriPharma Health Systems

AfriPharma Health Systems is pioneering pharmaceutical manufacturing in Africa, establishing WHO Prequalification-aligned facilities to address the region's critical dependence on imported medicines.

Our Mission

To strengthen pharmaceutical sovereignty across Africa by manufacturing high-quality, WHO-prequalified essential medicines locally, reducing import dependency and ensuring sustainable access to life-saving treatments for millions.

Strategic Vision

We are establishing state-of-the-art sterile injectables facilities in key African locations, starting with Mozambique and expanding to Botswana, with a focus on essential medicines that address infectious diseases, pain management, and pediatric care.

Why AfriPharma

  • WHO GMP-compliant manufacturing facility
  • Strategic location in Nacala SEZ, Mozambique
  • 7 WHO PQ-ready essential medicine products
  • 21.6M vials annual production capacity
  • Targeting SADC regional market
  • 73% gross margin business model
  • Lean manufacturing approach
  • Strong ESG and impact focus

Our Product Portfolio

Seven WHO Essential Medicines List products addressing critical healthcare needs across infectious diseases, pain management, and pediatric care

Analgesics

  • Paracetamol IV 1000mg/100mL (Adult)
  • Paracetamol IV 150mg/15mL (Pediatric)

Our unique pediatric paracetamol formulation addresses an $82M market gap, providing safe and effective pain management for children across the SADC region.

Antibiotics

  • Gentamicin 80mg/2mL Injectable
  • Metronidazole 500mg/100mL IV Infusion
  • Ceftriaxone 1g Injectable

Essential antibiotics addressing the most common bacterial infections, aligned with WHO treatment guidelines and national formularies.

Critical Care

  • Diazepam 10mg/2mL Injectable
  • Adrenaline 1mg/1mL Injectable

Life-saving emergency medications for acute care settings, seizure management, and anaphylactic reactions.
